Who I Am

Championing every learner's journey

I aim to support students to reach their full potential as successful, empowered learners within an inclusive educational environment.

Every child is unique and may have different learning styles. Our role, as educators, relies on facilitating and ensuring our learners grow and enjoy their learning journey.

I work closely with students, teachers, parents, and the wider school community — through workshops, co-teaching, ILP/SSP development, and professional development — to build truly inclusive spaces where every child belongs.

Inclusion is an Act.
